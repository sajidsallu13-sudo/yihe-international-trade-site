# YIHE FAQ

## General Questions

**Q: What is YIHE?**
A: YIHE International Trade Services is an import/export company connecting Chinese suppliers with global buyers. We handle sourcing, quality control, shipping, and customs for end-to-end supply chain management.

**Q: How long have you been in business?**
A: We have over 15 years combined experience in international trade and import/export operations.

**Q: Which countries do you serve?**
A: We ship to 150+ countries worldwide. Common destinations include USA, EU, UK, Canada, Australia, and Middle East.

## Services & Pricing

**Q: What services do you offer?**
A: Supplier sourcing, product sampling, quality inspections, production management, shipping/logistics, customs clearance, compliance consulting, and advisory services.

**Q: How much does it cost?**
A: Pricing varies by service. Our fee structure includes:
- Supplier sourcing: $500+
- Sampling: $1,000+
- Production management: 3-5% of order value
- QC inspection: $300+ per shipment
- Shipping: Market rates
- Customs clearance: $200+

**Q: Do you have minimum order quantities?**
A: MOQs depend on the supplier and product. We can often find suppliers for smaller runs ($500-$2,000 orders) though quantities of 100+ units are more cost-effective.

**Q: How long does the process take?**
A: Typical timelines:
- Supplier matching: 3-7 days
- Sampling: 2-4 weeks
- Production: 4-12 weeks (varies by product)
- Shipping: 1-4 weeks (depends on method)
- Total: 8-16 weeks typically

## Supplier Sourcing

**Q: How do you verify suppliers?**
A: We conduct background checks, facility audits, certification verification, and reference checks. All our suppliers hold ISO or equivalent certifications.

**Q: Can you find suppliers for any product?**
A: We specialize in electronics, textiles, home goods, industrial parts, and consumer products. Contact us for your specific needs.

**Q: What if I don't like the supplier recommendation?**
A: We can provide alternatives. We match multiple options so you can compare.

## Quality & Compliance

**Q: How do you ensure product quality?**
A: Through third-party inspections, random sampling, lab testing, and pre-shipment QC checks. We use independent inspectors for unbiased verification.

**Q: What certifications do you handle?**
A: CE marking (EU), FCC (USA), RoHS, REACH, ISO, and country-specific requirements. Our compliance experts guide you through each requirement.

**Q: Can you handle custom certifications?**
A: Yes. If your product requires specific certifications, we coordinate with testing labs and regulatory bodies.

## Shipping & Logistics

**Q: What shipping options are available?**
A: Sea freight (FCL/LCL), air freight, and express delivery. We recommend options based on your timeline and budget.

**Q: How much does shipping typically cost?**
A: Sea freight (1,000 kg Shanghai→NY): $800-$1,200
Air freight (1,000 kg Shanghai→NY): $4,000-$6,000
Costs vary based on weight, dimensions, destination, and market rates.

**Q: Do you include insurance?**
A: Cargo insurance is available and recommended for high-value shipments. Costs typically 1-3% of cargo value.

**Q: What about customs and duties?**
A: We handle documentation and coordinate with customs brokers. Import duties depend on product type and destination country (tariff codes). We provide estimates upfront.

## Account & Communication

**Q: How do I track my shipment?**
A: You receive real-time tracking information via email. You can also log into our portal (future feature) to monitor status.

**Q: Who is my point of contact?**
A: Each client is assigned a dedicated account manager who handles all communication and updates.

**Q: What languages do you speak?**
A: English, Mandarin Chinese, and Spanish. We can accommodate other languages with advance notice.

**Q: What are your hours?**
A: We operate during standard business hours. For urgent matters, contact our emergency line.

## Support & Issues

**Q: What if there's a quality issue?**
A: We investigate, document the problem, and work with the supplier to resolve it (replacement, refund, or compensation depending on agreement).

**Q: What if customs delays my shipment?**
A: We work with customs brokers to resolve delays. Clear documentation typically clears customs quickly (1-3 days).

**Q: Do you offer refunds?**
A: Service refunds depend on the specific agreement. We're committed to satisfaction and work to resolve any issues before considering refunds.

## Getting Started

**Q: How do I start?**
A: Contact us with your product details and requirements. We'll schedule a consultation to discuss your needs and create a customized plan.

**Q: Is there a consultation fee?**
A: Initial consultation is free. We only charge for services as you proceed.

**Q: What information do you need from me?**
A: Product specifications, target quantities, budget, timeline, destination country, and any specific requirements (certifications, quality standards, etc.).

**Q: Can you work with my existing suppliers?**
A: Yes. We can provide QC, shipping, and logistics services for your existing relationships.
